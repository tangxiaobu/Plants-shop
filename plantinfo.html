Pitcher Plant

$28.99

The Pitcher Plant is one of the most fascinating carnivorous plants that can attract anyone’s attention in an instant. A great conversation piece, it flaunts fleshy funnels or pitcher-like tubes that trap insects and small animals. The elongated, pitcher-like traps, in fact, are the plant’s specialized leaves. It's the perfect plant if you're looking to add an exotic plant display in your home, office, or garden.

Description Plant Care Guide
Common Name: Tropical pitcher plant, monkey cups Scientific Name: Nepenthes spp. Water: Constantly moist
Light: Partial sun or bright, indirect light
